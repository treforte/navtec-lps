Common Questions

Island in the Sky is the most accessible district, with dramatic mesa-top overlooks and the Shafer Trail — great for half-day and first-time visits. The Needles is more remote, known for spires and multi-day camping trips. The Maze is Canyonlands' most remote and rugged district, reached via long backcountry drives — best suited to travelers wanting true desert solitude.

We'll confirm exact inclusions for your selected tour when you check availability — some tours include entrance fees in the trip rate, others don't. National Park entrance fees are waived with an annual pass.

It varies by tour — Canyonlands Half-Day and Arches Half-Day involve minimal hiking, while White Rim, Chesler Park, and Horseshoe Canyon include guided hikes ranging from easy to moderate. Tell us your group's fitness level on the form below and we'll recommend the right tour.

All camping gear, meals, and guiding are included on overnight and multi-day trips like the Needles 3-Day and Maze 4-Day camps — you bring personal items and we'll send a full packing list once you book.
