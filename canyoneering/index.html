Yes — Robbers Roost, Moab Canyoneering, and Granary Canyon are all explicitly guided as beginner-friendly, even though some carry a "Strenuous" difficulty rating for the terrain itself. Your guide teaches every technique on-site; no prior experience is required.

Easy trips involve minimal technical rope work. Moderate trips include multiple rappels and some down-climbing. Strenuous trips involve longer days, more demanding rappels, and require solid physical fitness — but as noted above, "Strenuous" doesn't rule out beginners when the trip is guided.

Closed-toe shoes you don't mind getting wet, quick-dry clothing, a water bottle, and sun protection. Several routes include water-filled potholes and possible swims, so plan to get wet. We provide all technical gear — harnesses, helmets, and ropes.
